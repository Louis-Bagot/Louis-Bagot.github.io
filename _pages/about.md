I am currently a Temporary Assistant Teacher-Researcher at Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1, teaching in the Informatics department and researching at laboratory LIRIS within the SyCoSMA team.  

Research
======
My main research interest is **Reinforcement Learning**, and in particular the self-supervised learning of general representations and behaviors which can be used to solve any task. 

Teaching
======
This 2026-2027 academic year I am responsible for the Machine Learning course in the Artificial Intelligence Master of the Informatics department of Université Lyon 1. If you are looking for information about this course, please check out the Teaching page.
